Things to see in Berlin

The Brandenburg Gate

Located in no-man's land between East and West Germany during the Cold War, this famous monument, built during the 18th-century reign of Friedrich Wilhelm II, has long been a defining symbol of Berlin and both the city's division and unification.

The Holocaust Memorial

Show your respects at the Memorial to the Murdered Jews of Europe, visit the information center to educate yourself on the events that occoured.

Things to do in Berlin

Visit Mauerpark

Mauerpark is a busy Sunday flea market, selling almost everything imaginable. It's also where Bearpit Karaoke, a popular outdoor singing session, occours.

Visit Schivelbeiner Straße

Berlin is home to not only the world’s largest vegan grocery store chain (Veganz), but also is home to the world’s first vegan avenue (Schivelbeiner Straße). View more info here!
